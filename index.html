The Highlights

I was born and raised here in Nashville.

I graduated from Belmont University in 2010 with a degree in English/Writing and French.

Shortly after I graduated I moved to Seoul, South Korea to put my degree to work by teaching English as a Second Language to middle schoolers.

After a year in Korea I realized teaching wasn't for me. Instead, I decided to pursue the entrepreneurial realm, fumbling through a couple different startups while working a day-job.

My interest in small business and technology lead me to apply to the Nashville Software School, where I am currently studying HTML, CSS, Javascript and C#/.Net.

I want to use my new skills to contribute measurable value back to the city that has shaped such a huge part of who I am, whether that is in the fields of education, health, music, culture or the sciences.
